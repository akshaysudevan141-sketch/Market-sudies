Methodology ▸

Core Formula

India Landed Price = (Intl. Price ÷ Unit) × USD/INR × (1 + Duty%)

Gold & Silver

Spot price via XAU/USD & XAG/USD → Convert troy oz to grams (÷ 31.1035) → Multiply by USD/INR → Apply 6% duty (5% BCD + 1% AIDC).

Purity: 22K = 24K × (22/24), 18K = 24K × (18/24)

10g Price: Per gram × 10

Base Metals

LME prices (USD/MT) via metals.live → Convert to ₹/kg (÷ 1000 × USD/INR) → Apply respective duty rates. Copper: 2.5%, Aluminium: 7.5%, Zinc: 5%, Lead: 5%, Nickel: 0%.

Energy

Crude Oil (WTI USD/barrel) → Apply ~5% duty. Natural Gas (USD/MMBtu) → Apply 2.5% duty. No unit conversion needed for standard Indian quotes.

Platinum

Spot price via XPT/USD → Convert troy oz to grams (÷ 31.1035) → Multiply by USD/INR → Apply 15.39% duty (12.5% BCD + Social Welfare Surcharge).

Data sources: Yahoo Finance (international futures), Metals.live (LME metals). Prices are calculated mathematically and are for illustrative purposes only.

IMPORTANT: This application does NOT display live data from NSE or MCX. All Indian quotes (including Standard Contract Equivalents) are mathematical approximations derived from international COMEX/NYMEX/LME benchmarks converted to INR via live forex rates.
